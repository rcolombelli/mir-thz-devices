The research team of Raffaele Colombelli is part of the OptoGan department at the Institute d'Electronique Fondamentale. We closely collaborate with the research team of Francois Julien. While we concentrate our efforts at mid-IR and THz wavelengths, Francois Julien's activity focuses on Intersubband Transitions at Telecom Wavelengths in Nitride-based Semiconductor Heterostructures. The two groups closely collaborate in order to maximize the sharing of scientific and technological know-how on III-V semiconductor heterostructures.

In addition, we have active collaborations with the following research groups in France and abroad:
